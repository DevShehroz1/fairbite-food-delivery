# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Customers (Food Orderers)
**Interests:** Affordable transparent pricing, fast delivery, wide selection, dietary accommodation  
**Influence:** HIGH | **Impact:** HIGH  
**Requirements:** Mobile-first UI, real-time tracking, multiple payment options, dietary filters, order history

### 2. Restaurant Owners
**Interests:** Lower commission, increased visibility, order tools, timely payments, analytics  
**Influence:** CRITICAL | **Impact:** CRITICAL  
**Requirements:** Menu management, order notifications, sales dashboard, 15% commission, flexible hours

### 3. Delivery Riders
**Interests:** Fair compensation, flexible hours, route optimization, timely payments  
**Influence:** HIGH | **Impact:** CRITICAL  
**Requirements:** Mobile order acceptance app, GPS navigation, earnings transparency, shift flexibility

### 4. System Administrators
**Interests:** System stability, user satisfaction, fraud prevention, operational efficiency  
**Influence:** HIGH | **Impact:** HIGH  
**Requirements:** Admin dashboard, user management, analytics, dispute resolution, system monitoring

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 5. Payment Gateway Providers (Stripe)
**Interests:** Transaction volume, integration reliability, security compliance  
**Requirements:** Secure processing, multiple methods, refund handling

### 6. Regulatory Authorities
**Interests:** Legal compliance, food safety, labor rights, tax compliance  
**Requirements:** Food safety certifications, labor law compliance, data privacy

### 7. Local Communities
**Interests:** Local business support, employment opportunities  
**Requirements:** Hyperlocal discovery, small business support

### 8. Academic Evaluators (Course Instructors)
**Interests:** SE principles demonstrated, technical competency, documentation quality  
**Requirements:** Complete SRS, UML diagrams, architecture docs, test reports, project management artifacts
