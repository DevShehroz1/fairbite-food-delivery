# Problem Statement

## Executive Summary
Current food delivery platforms (Foodpanda, Careem NOW, UberEats) operate with unsustainable business models that charge excessive commissions (25-35%), under-compensate riders, hide fees from customers, and exclude small local restaurants.

## Problem Analysis

### 1. High Commission Burden on Restaurants
- **Current state:** 25-35% industry-standard commission per order
- **Impact:** Restaurant profit margins reduced 40-60%; small/local eateries pushed out; menu prices artificially inflated

### 2. Unfair Rider Compensation
- **Current state:** Low per-delivery earnings, no benefits, penalty-based systems
- **Impact:** High rider turnover, service degradation, social welfare concerns

### 3. Price Transparency Issues
- **Current state:** Hidden fees revealed at checkout; surge pricing unexplained
- **Impact:** Customer distrust, cart abandonment, negative UX

### 4. Limited Dietary Options
- **Current state:** Only basic vegetarian filter; no allergen info; halal verification unclear
- **Impact:** Users with dietary restrictions underserved

## Competitive Gaps

| Platform | Commission | Rider Fair Pay | Dietary Filters | Transparency |
|----------|-----------|---------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Foodpanda | 30% | No | Basic | Low |
| Careem NOW | 25% | No | Basic | Medium |
| UberEats | 30% | No | Basic | Medium |
| **FairBite** | **15%** | **Yes** | **Advanced** | **Full** |

## Proposed Solution: FairBite
- **15% commission rate** — nearly half the industry standard
- **Fair rider compensation** — transparent wage calculator built in
- **Full price transparency** — itemized breakdown before checkout
- **Advanced dietary filtering** — Halal, Vegan, Allergen-free, Keto, Gluten-free
- **Hyperlocal focus** — prioritize neighborhood restaurants

## Success Criteria
1. 80%+ of restaurants report improved profitability vs. previous platforms
2. Riders meet minimum earnings guarantee
3. 90%+ of customers rate pricing as transparent
4. Year 1 targets: 500+ restaurants, 10,000+ active users
